Booking

Born as a promoter, from 2010 until 2015 we organized shows in Barcelona. In that period we booked national and international acts, such as: Amen Dunes, Barn Owl, Bill Orcutt, Bitchin' Bajas, Blues Control, Cam Deas, CAVE, Cian Nugent, Coàgul, Daniel Bachman, Ensemble Economique, Excepter, Gala Drop, ITAL, Kikagaku Moyo, Larry Gus, Lucrecia Dalt, Magik Markers, Mammane Sani, Manta, Maria Minerva, Max Tundra, Mdou Moctar, Mike Cooper, Norberto Lobo, Ocellot, Pega Monstro, Pharmakon, Psychic Ills, Purling Hiss, Rayo-60, Ryley Walker, Sightings, Sir Richard Bishop, Spacin', Steve Gunn, Steve Hauschildt, Summer Recreation Camp, Sun Araw & Laraaji, Sun Araw, Sun Color, Sunny Graves, Torn Hawk, Toro y Moi, Tropa Macaca, Tsembla, Tyvek, WANDA GROUP

Artistic Direction and Production


Portugal Alive is an annual series of events organized in collaboration with the Portuguese Consulate and Embassy in Spain. Every hear the event takes place in Barcelona and Madrid:

  • 2014 : Dead Combo + B Fachada
  • 2015 : Linda Martini + Capicua + Noiserv
  • 2016 : X‐Wife + Da Chick + peixe : avião
  • 2017 : Gala Drop + Sensible Soccers + Pega Monstro
  • 2018 : Capicua + Bruno Pernadas + Surma
  • 2019 : Conan Osiris + Whales + Reis da República
